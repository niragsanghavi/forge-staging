Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026
The short version

Forge stores your name, your workout logs, and which groups you're in. That's the app.

We don't sell your data. We don't run ads. We don't track your location, read your contacts, or ask for your photos.

You can ask us to delete everything at any time, and we will.

The rest of this page is the detailed version, in plain language.

1Who we are

Forge is a fitness accountability app operated from Mumbai, India, by an individual developer. In this policy, "Forge", "we", and "us" mean the operator of the Forge app and the website at goforge.in.

If you have any question about your data, you can reach a real person directly at team@goforge.in.

2What we collect

Only what the app needs to work. Here's the complete list:

WhatWhy we need it
Your display name So your group knows who logged a workout. Usually set by your group admin when they add you, or chosen by you when you join.
Your workout logs The type of workout (gym, running, yoga, etc.) and the date it happened. This is the core of the app — it's what gets scored.
Your group membership Which groups you belong to, which team you're on, and your role in the group.
Your PIN Stored only as a scrambled (hashed) value, never as the number itself. Used to confirm it's you when you open the app.
An anonymous device ID Automatically generated by Google Firebase to keep you signed in on your device. It isn't linked to your Google account or your real identity.
Basic usage data Which screens get opened, and roughly how the app is used. This tells us what to improve. It's aggregate, not surveillance.
Device type Whether you're on mobile or desktop, and general browser information, so the app renders correctly.

What we don't collect

  • Your email address or phone number (unless you choose to email or message us — then we obviously have it)
  • Your location, at any level of precision
  • Your photos, camera, microphone, or contacts
  • Any payment information — Forge is currently free and processes no payments
  • Detailed health data — no heart rate, weight, body measurements, or anything from a wearable. We know you did a workout on a date. That's all.

3Why we collect it

Your data is used for exactly these purposes and nothing else:

  • To run the app — showing your logs, calculating points, building the leaderboard, tracking streaks and seasons
  • To show your group what you did — that's the entire point of a shared accountability app. Your name and workouts are visible to the other members of your groups
  • To keep you signed in — the anonymous device ID and your hashed PIN
  • To improve the app — aggregate usage patterns tell us which features work and which don't

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making, and we never sell it.

4Who can see your data

Other people in your groups

Your name, your workout logs, your points, and your streak are visible to other members of any group you're in. This is intentional — Forge is a group accountability app, and that visibility is the product. Don't log anything you wouldn't want your group to see.

Service providers

We use a small number of third-party services to run Forge. They process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase (Google LLC) — stores the database, handles sign-in, and provides analytics. Firebase privacy information
  • Google Analytics (Google LLC) — measures usage of our public web pages only (the goforge.in/about page and any surveys). Google's privacy policy
  • GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.) — serves the website files themselves

Nobody else

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to anyone, for any purpose, ever. We do not share it with advertisers or data brokers. The only other circumstance in which we would disclose data is if we were legally required to by a valid order under Indian law.

5Where your data lives

Your data is stored on Google Cloud servers located in Mumbai, India (the asia-south1 region). Your workout history and personal data stay within India.

Two small exceptions, for completeness: our website files are served by GitHub Pages via a global content network, and our public web pages use Google Analytics, which processes aggregate usage data on Google's international infrastructure. Neither of these handles your workout data or your identity.

6How long we keep it

  • Your workout logs and season history are kept for as long as your group is active, because past seasons are part of the app — your history and archives depend on them.
  • Deleted (voided) logs are marked as removed rather than erased immediately, so that group scores stay consistent. They no longer count toward anything and are not shown.
  • Usage analytics are retained according to Google's standard analytics retention periods.
  • If you ask us to delete your data, we remove it — see the next section.

7Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what we hold about you — ask, and we'll send you everything
  • Correct it — if your name or a log is wrong, we'll fix it
  • Delete it — see below
  • Complain — if you think we've mishandled your data, tell us first, and you also have the right to raise it with India's Data Protection Board

There's no form and no ticket system. Email a person: team@goforge.in

8Deleting your data

To delete your Forge data, email team@goforge.in with the subject "Delete my data", and tell us your display name and which group you're in.

We'll delete your personal data and confirm to you when it's done, normally within 7 days and always within 30.

What gets deleted: your name, your identity record, your PIN, your group membership, and your personal workout history.

What may remain: a group's past season results may retain an anonymised record that a team scored certain points, because removing it would rewrite other people's history. This record will not identify you.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you rejoin later, you start fresh.

9Children

Forge is not intended for children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect data from them.

Forge groups are often family groups, and a group admin may add a family member who is a minor. If you are under 18, you should only use Forge with the knowledge and permission of a parent or guardian. If you're a parent and want your child's data removed, email team@goforge.in and we'll delete it, no questions asked.

10Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data: connections are encrypted (HTTPS), your PIN is stored scrambled rather than in plain text, and database access is restricted by security rules.

Being honest with you: Forge is a small, actively developed app built by one person, not a bank. The data it holds is your name and your workouts. It doesn't hold anything financially sensitive. We're continuously improving our security, including moving toward proper account-based sign-in. Please don't reuse a PIN that you use for anything important, like your phone or your bank card.

If you ever discover a security problem in Forge, please tell us at team@goforge.in — you'll get a fast, grateful reply.

11Surveys and the public website

Occasionally we run in-app or link-based surveys. Survey responses are collected anonymously — we do not store your name or any identifier alongside your answers, and we cannot tell who submitted which response.

Our public pages (goforge.in/about and similar) use Google Analytics to measure basic engagement — how many people visit and which sections they read. This is standard web analytics and does not identify you personally.

12Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle your data, we'll update this page and change the "last updated" date at the top. If a change is significant — for example, if we started collecting something new — we'll tell you in the app before it takes effect.

13Contact

For any privacy question, request, or complaint, including data access and deletion requests:

Email: team@goforge.in

We aim to respond within 7 days.
