How to Use Forge
1
Join your group

Get the 6-character code from your group's organiser, punch it in, and pick your name from the roster.

2
Log a workout

Hit the Log tab, pick the day, tap what you did (or type your own), and lock it in. That's it — five seconds, done.

3
Check the leaderboard

See where your team stands and where you rank against everyone else. Use it as motivation, or as a reason to text the group some trash talk.

4
Set your monthly goal

Tap the ring on your home screen and commit to a number of workouts for the month. A target beats a vibe.

5
Take The Iron Pledge

Feeling brave? Opt in, pledge your weekly target, and play for double-or-nothing. Glory if you hit it, a zeroed week if you don't.

6
Running the group? Visit Admin

If you're the admin, the Admin tab is your control room — assign teams and roles, flip on monthly twists, and manage the season.
