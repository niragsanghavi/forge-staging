THE THREE IMMOVABLE GATES
1. The 14-day clock — Google requires a personal dev account's app to run closed testing with 12+ testers for 14 straight days before production access can even be requested. Start by Aug 16 or Sep 1 slips day-for-day.
2. Apple review — plan for at least one rejection (thin-wrapper "minimum functionality" pushback is common for web-wrapped apps). October submission exists so November can absorb resubmissions.
3. Sign in with Apple — the moment Google Sign-In exists in the iOS app, Apple mandates their sign-in too. It is scaffolded in September, not discovered in October.
